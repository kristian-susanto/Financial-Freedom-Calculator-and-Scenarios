Formula Overview

Standard Base Formula
FF = E × 12 × (1 + i)t - 1 i
Golden Age Formula
FF = E × (1 + i)(A - (Yc - Yb)) × 12 × (1 + i)t - 1 i

Where :

  • FF = Financial Freedom
  • E = Monthly Living Expenses
  • i = Annual Inflation Rate
  • t = Fund Period (Years)
  • A = Age Target | Yc = Current Year | Yb = Year of Birth

Scenario Table Logic:

LEann = IF × E × 12 FF
FFann = InvF × [ 1 + ( 1 + i 1 - rLE - 1 ) ]

Row 1 Base Mapping:

  • Initial Fund = Financial Freedom
  • Living Expenses = Initial Fund × (Expenses × 12) / Financial Freedom
  • Investment Fund = Initial Fund - Living Expenses
  • Final Fund = Investment Fund × (1 + ((1 + Inflation) / (1 - Living Expenses Rate) - 1))

Row 2+ Compound Sequence:

  • Initial Fund = Previous Row Final Fund
  • Inflation = (Current Living Expenses / Previous Living Expenses) - 1

Where :

  • LEann = Annual Living Expenses

  • FF = Financial Freedom
  • FFann = Annual Projected Final Fund
  • InvF = Investment Fund
